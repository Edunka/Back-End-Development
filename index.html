Welcome Parents,Teachers,Students

Rishton Academy Primary School is a public school located in the town of Rishton in the county of Lancashire, England. The school was established in the early 1900s, originally as a small local school catering to the needs of the growing population in the area. Over time, the school expanded to accommodate a larger student body and became known for its excellent academic programs.

The purpose of Rishton Academy Primary School is to provide quality education to children in the community. The school aims to instill a love of learning in its students, and to provide them with the skills and knowledge they need to succeed in their academic and personal lives. The school also places a strong emphasis on character development, promoting values such as respect, responsibility, and kindness.

Throughout its history, Rishton Academy Primary School has undergone many changes and updates to keep pace with the changing educational landscape. Today, the school is known for its innovative approach to teaching and its commitment to providing a nurturing, supportive environment for all students. The school has a strong track record of academic success, with many of its graduates going on to attend top universities and pursue successful careers in a variety of fields.

Overall, Rishton Academy Primary School has a rich history of providing quality education to generations of students in the Rishton community. Through its commitment to excellence and dedication to its students, the school continues to play a vital role in the education of young people in Lancashire and beyond.
